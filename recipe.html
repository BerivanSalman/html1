Sweet cheese pancakes

A light and gently sweet breakfast dish or a dessert for picky eaters. With crispy coating and the light and soft inside, you get a lovely balance of flavors and textures.These delicious pancakes pair perfectly with sour cream and fruit in pretty much any form.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Cheese

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients
  2. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and add a small amount of oil to coat the surface.
  3. Using a spoon, drop small portions of the batter onto the pan, forming small pancakes. Cook until golden brown on both sides.
  4. Serve warm with sour cream and your favorite fruit preserves or fresh fruit.
